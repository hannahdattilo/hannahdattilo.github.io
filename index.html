Hello!

My name is Hannah Dattilo and I am a fourth year PhD Candidate in Electrical Engineering at Vanderbilt University working with Dr. Robert Reed. I received my B.S in Physics from Oglethorpe University in Atlanta, GA and my M.S. in Physics from Fisk University in Nashville, TN through the Fisk-Vanderbilt Master's-to-PhD Bridge program.
